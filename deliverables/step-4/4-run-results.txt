What did you produce?

The model successfully completed a zero-shot forecasting run on the ETTh1 dataset. Using 512 past time steps as input, it predicted the next 64 future time steps. The run achieved an MSE of 0.3556 and an MAE of 0.3623. Since lower MSE and MAE indicate better forecasting performance, these values show the model produced a valid forecast with measurable prediction error.

Specifically, the model produced:
    Mean Absolute Error: its average error was about 0.3623 in MAE terms
    Mean Squared Error: its squared-error-based metric was 0.3556 in MSE terms
